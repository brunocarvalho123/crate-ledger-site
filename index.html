About Crate Ledger

Crate Ledger is a simple, elegant way to track your wealth. Designed for individuals who want clarity and control, it supports a wide range of asset types: crypto, stocks, ETFs, bonds, cash, metals, and more. Whether you're tracking a few stocks or a diversified portfolio, Crate Ledger gives you the tools to stay on top of it - all without needing an account or internet connection.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add assets to my portfolio?
Tap the "+" button to add new assets. You can choose from crypto, stocks, ETFs, bonds, cash, precious metals, or create custom assets. The app supports automatic price updates for most asset types.
Does the app require an internet connection?
No! Crate Ledger works completely offline. While internet is needed for real-time price updates, you can manually enter and track all your assets without any connection.
Is my financial data secure and private?
Absolutely. Your data stays completely on your device - no accounts required, no ads, no tracking. Optional iCloud sync keeps your data backed up across your Apple devices while maintaining privacy.
Can I track assets in different currencies?
Yes! You can input asset values in your preferred currency (USD, EUR, etc.) and the app will handle conversions for your total portfolio overview.
How do I view my portfolio performance?
The app provides performance graphs to visualize your portfolio's evolution over time, plus a complete overview showing your total net worth and breakdown by asset type.
Can I export my portfolio data?
Right now you can't but in the future yes, you will be able to export your portfolio data for backup purposes or to use with other financial tools.
