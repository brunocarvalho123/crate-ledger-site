Crate Ledger was built from the ground up with your privacy in mind. Your financial data never leaves your device unless you choose to sync with iCloud.

Data Collection

Crate Ledger collects ZERO personal data. We believe your financial information should remain completely private and under your control.

No Personal Information

We don't collect names, emails, addresses, or any identifying information

No Financial Data Collection

Your portfolio holdings, asset values, and financial data stay on your device

No Usage Analytics

We don't track how you use the app or collect behavioral data

Data Storage

All your portfolio and asset data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure data storage mechanisms. This means:

  • Your data is encrypted and protected by your device's security
  • Only you have access to your financial information
  • Data persists even when offline
  • No external servers store your information

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable iCloud sync, your data is backed up to your personal iCloud account:

  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Only accessible through your Apple ID
  • Synced across your Apple devices only
  • You can disable this feature at any time
  • We have no access to your iCloud data

Third-Party Services

For price updates, Crate Ledger may fetch current market prices from financial data providers. However:

  • No personal information is sent with price requests
  • Requests are anonymous and don't include your holdings
  • Price data is fetched generically (e.g., "Bitcoin price") without context
  • The app works completely offline if you prefer manual price entry

No Tracking or Analytics

Crate Ledger contains:

  • No advertising networks or trackers
  • No analytics or usage monitoring
  • No crash reporting that includes personal data
  • No behavioral tracking or profiling
  • No social media integrations

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, you automatically have:

  • Complete control over your financial information
  • The right to use the app without sharing any personal data
  • Freedom to export or delete your data at any time
  • No need to request data deletion - it's already private
